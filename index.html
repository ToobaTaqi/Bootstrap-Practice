Frozen (2013):

Cast and their Traits

Anna(Kristen Bell)

Anna is the younger sister of Elsa and is known for her cheerful and optimistic personality. She is determined to mend her relationship with Elsa and restore their sisterly bond. Anna is also portrayed as a selfless and courageous character who risks her own safety to protect the people she loves.

Elsa(Idina Menzel)

Elsa is the older sister of Anna and has the power to create and control ice and snow. She is initially fearful and hesitant to use her powers, but overcomes her fears and embraces them in the song "Let It Go". Elsa is portrayed as a kind, caring, and protective sister who would do anything to keep Anna safe.

Olaf(Josh Gad)

Olaf is a snowman created by Elsa's magic and is known for his humorous and innocent personality. He is curious and childlike, often asking questions and seeking new experiences. Olaf is also very loyal to his friends and is willing to risk his own safety to help them.

Kristoff(Jonathan Groff)

TKristoff is an ice harvester who becomes Anna's love interest. He is portrayed as a rugged and independent character who is skilled at survival in the wilderness. Despite his rough exterior, Kristoff is also shown to have a kind and caring heart, especially towards his reindeer, Sven.

Hans(Santino Fontana)

Hans from Frozen 1 is characterized as "manipulative." He portrays himself as charming and kind but is revealed to be deceitful, conniving, and a master manipulator. He gains the trust of Anna and Elsa, but ultimately betrays them for his own gain, proving his true nature as a manipulative character.

Frozen 2:

Cast and their Traits

Anna(Kristen Bell)

Anna is still portrayed as a cheerful and optimistic character, but also becomes more assertive and confident in Frozen 2. She is determined to support Elsa on her journey and protect their kingdom from danger.

Elsa(Idina Menzel)

In Frozen 2, Elsa continues to struggle with her powers and her place in the world. She sets out on a journey to discover the truth about her past and the source of her powers. Elsa is still portrayed as a caring and protective sister, but also undergoes a journey of self-discovery and learns to embrace her true identity.

Olaf(Josh Gad)

Olaf continues to provide comic relief in Frozen 2, but also faces some existential questions about life and change. He is still portrayed as a loyal and supportive friend to Elsa and Anna.

Kristoff(Jonathan Groff)

Kristoff is still portrayed as a rugged and independent character, but also becomes more vulnerable and emotional in Frozen 2. He struggles to express his feelings towards Anna and sings a heartfelt ballad called "Lost in the Woods".

Sven

Sven is still Kristoff's loyal and dependable companion, and even gets his own song called "Reindeer(s) are Better Than People (Cont.)". He also plays an important role in the plot by helping to transport the characters across the enchanted forest.
